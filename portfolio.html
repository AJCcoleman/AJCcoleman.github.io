Alex Coleman

I am a PhD student at the University of Kent, in the Programming Languages and Systems (PLAS) and the Institute of Cyber Security for Society (iCSS) research groups, School of Computing. Supervised by Dominic Orchard and Vineet Rajani (external expert and previously my supervisor at Kent).

Research Focus

My research focuses on secure information flow, primarily on developing graded modalities to capture all dimensions of declassification for safe and secure information disclosure. I am conducting this work at the source level by designing a statically typed higher-order language, utilising semantic models using logical relations to enforce security guarantees.
